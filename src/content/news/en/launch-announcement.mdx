## Establishment Announcement

In December 2024, TONYA Inc. was established to support EC/D2C brands entering physical retail markets in Japan.

EC and D2C brands that have grown online face many barriers when expanding into physical retail. TONYA serves as a "new wholesaler" that translates brand language into retail language, providing end-to-end support from strategy design to buyer negotiations, store placement, and POS analytics.

We look forward to working with you.
