## Press Release

Our SNS x in-store promotion service **Tagpo** has been adopted as an official promotion program by the Welcia Group and Tsuruha Group.

Tagpo leverages a trend-savvy member network to drive in-store purchases and SNS posts simultaneously at target stores. By combining POS data with SNS data, we accelerate in-store sales and expand digital awareness at the same time.

We will continue to propose new forms of promotion in the retail market.
